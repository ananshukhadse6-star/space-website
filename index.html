Popular Nebula Information

Crab Nebula (M1): Remnant of a supernova explosion in 1054 AD, containing a pulsar emitting powerful radiation. - provided by high level intelligence
Eagle Nebula (M16): Famous for the “Pillars of Creation,” a region of active star formation. - provided by high level intelligence
Carina Nebula: One of the largest star-forming regions in our galaxy, home to massive stars like Eta Carinae. - provided by high level intelligence
Ring Nebula (M57): A planetary nebula representing the dying stages of a medium-sized star. - provided by high level intelligence
